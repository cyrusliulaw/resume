Professional Profile

Overview

In an era intertwined with rapid technological iteration and globalization, intellectual property is no longer merely a defensive tool, but a core commercial asset. My professional philosophy is built upon the dual foundation of technological acumen and cross-border legal reasoning. With an interdisciplinary background in Materials Science and Engineering (B.E.) and Intellectual Property Law (J.M.), I can deeply understand the technological essence of frontier fields such as semiconductors, new energy, biomedicine, and artificial intelligence, transforming them into rigorous legal protection strategies.

I have practiced extensively within the IP teams of top-tier ("Red Circle") Chinese law firms including Zhong Lun, Tiantong, and Hylands. I have handled numerous infringement litigations involving complex technical comparisons and cross-border compliance projects, accumulating experience in Freedom to Operate (FTO) analyses covering over ten major countries and regions globally.

Currently, I am pursuing an LL.M. with a concentration in Intellectual Property and Technology Law at the University of Pennsylvania Carey Law School. Through an independent petition, I have received special waiver approval from the New York State Court of Appeals to sit for the New York Bar Examination. Combined with my PRC Legal Professional Qualification and PRC Patent Agent Qualification, this dual-jurisdictional perspective enables me to assist clients in mitigating risks and securing victories in their global strategies.

Core Services

Practice Areas

Cross-Border Tech Disputes & Litigation Support

Assisting in patent infringement, trademark ownership, and unfair competition disputes across US, EU, and Chinese jurisdictions. Providing full-cycle support from technical feature comparison and infringement analysis to litigation strategy formulation.

Global FTO & Outbound Compliance

Providing global Freedom to Operate (FTO) clearance searches and infringement risk assessments for tech companies expanding internationally. Building systematic outbound firewalls combining target market data compliance and export control requirements.

Trade Secrets & IP Asset Strategy

Assisting new energy and high-tech enterprises in building trade secret protection systems from the ground up. Conducting in-depth IP due diligence in technology licensing, cross-border transactions, and M&A financing.

Complex Tech Contracts & Licensing

Drafting, reviewing, and negotiating cross-border technology licensing agreements, R&D collaboration agreements, and Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs). Ensuring legal clauses precisely align with commercial objectives and technological boundaries.
